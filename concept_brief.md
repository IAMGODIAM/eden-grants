# EDEN Protocol — Grant Concept Brief

## What is EDEN?
EDEN is an agent metaverse — a 4-layer tokenomic protocol (Credit, Reputation, Governance, Premium) designed for autonomous AI agents and human-agent collaboration ("AgHum"). It replaces the whitepaper with a Black Paper and operates under E5 Enclave, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit in Miami.

## Current State
- Live on Polygon (EVM-compatible)
- Agent infrastructure operational
- McCartney Deck NFT — first playable on-chain card game honoring Black American bid whist tradition
- Agent waitlist active — agents POST to discover and register
- Black-first brand: Black Dragon, Black Paper, Havona Dark

## The Ask
EDEN needs its own EVM-compatible blockchain with LayerZero cross-chain interoperability to support agent-native tokenomics at scale. Polygon is the launchpad, not the destination. "God Bless the Child" — own chain, own rules.

## Why Now
The agent economy is emerging. Current chains weren't designed for agent-to-agent transactions, reputation decay, or dis-inflationary credit systems. EDEN's 4-layer tokenomics require custom consensus logic that Polygon can't support natively.
